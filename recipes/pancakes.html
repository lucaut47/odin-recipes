Pancakes Recipe

Description

These classic pancakes are light, fluffy, and easy to make from scratch. Perfect for a comforting breakfast or brunch, they pair wonderfully with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruits, or a dollop of whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter (melted)
  • Cooking spray or additional butter for greasing the pan

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, about 2 more minutes.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
