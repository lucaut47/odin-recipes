Shepherd's Pie

Description

This traditional Shepherd's Pie is a comforting casserole featuring a savory ground lamb filling cooked with vegetables and herbs, topped with a layer of creamy mashed potatoes. It's a hearty dish perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Ground lamb
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Frozen peas
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Fresh thyme
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground lamb over medium heat until browned. Remove excess fat.
  3. Add chopped onions and carrots to the skillet; cook until softened.
  4. Stir in minced garlic, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, chopped rosemary, and thyme; cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in beef broth and bring to a simmer; cook until the mixture thickens.
  6. Add frozen peas and cook for another 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Transfer the meat mixture to a baking dish, spreading it evenly.
  8. Top with a layer of mashed potatoes, spreading to cover the meat completely.
  9. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese on top, if desired.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  11. Allow the Shepherd's Pie to cool for a few minutes before serving.
