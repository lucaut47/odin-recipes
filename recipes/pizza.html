Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza features a classic crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a family dinner or gathering with friends.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • White sugar
  • Warm water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. In a bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water; let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in flour, salt, and olive oil. Beat until smooth. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a round. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
  5. Spread tomato sauce over the crust, and top with mozzarella cheese and your chosen toppings.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly, about 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
